Getting Started

What's included in the free trial? ⌄

Full access to all iris mapping systems, image uploads, analysis tools, and export features for 14 days. No credit card required.

Which iris mapping systems are included? ⌄

All plans include 8 professional mapping systems: Angerer, Bourdiol, IrisLAB, Jaussas, Jensen, and Roux systems in multiple languages.

Do I need to download anything? ⌄

No downloads or installations required. IrisMapper Pro runs in any web browser on Windows, Mac, tablets, and mobile devices.

Plans & Billing

Can I switch plans later? ⌄

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What makes Clinic different from Practitioner? ⌄

The Clinic plan includes everything in Practitioner plus multi-user access (up to 5 team members), priority support, and advanced practice management features.

How do I cancel my subscription? ⌄

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. You'll maintain full access until your billing period ends.

What's your money-back guarantee? ⌄

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on annual and 2-year plans.

Security & Technical

How secure is my patient data? ⌄

We use enterprise-grade SSL encryption and secure cloud storage. Your data is completely private and GDPR compliant. We never share or sell your information.
